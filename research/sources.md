A curated list of 10 practitioners who have directly shaped how I understand 
SEO, content strategy, and AI-powered content production.

## 1. Collin SEO (Alex Collins Alonso)
**Role:** SEO Consultant and Educator  
**YouTube:** https://www.youtube.com/@COLLINSEO  
**Collect:** YouTube transcripts  

Alex Collins Alonso is the creator I credit most for teaching me what 
actually makes content rank. His channel takes a psychological and analytical 
approach to SEO — he only shares strategies he has personally tested with 
clients, and he documents the process in detail. He provided free worksheets 
and tutorials on keyword research, outline creation, content clustering, and 
semantic positioning that gave me a foundational understanding of how search 
engines evaluate content quality.

His video "How To Use ChatGPT And AI To Create SEO Pages (The 'Google 
Friendly' Way)" was one of the first pieces of content that helped me 
understand how to use AI in a way that aligns with what Google actually 
rewards. His keyword research video "How To Find Keywords For SEO (New 
Out-Of-The-Box Technique)" changed how I approach topic discovery entirely. 
Despite having fewer subscribers than mainstream SEO channels, the depth and 
applicability of his content is unmatched in my experience. Everything he 
teaches is tried and tested — I have applied it directly and seen results.
